Welcome to DiaCare

DiaCare is your comprehensive diabetes management ecosystem designed to help you track your health, learn about diabetes, and connect with a supportive community.

Main Features

  • Glucose & Insulin Tracking
  • Smart Reminders
  • Carb Counting
  • Prescription Management
  • Care Team Sharing
  • AI Insights & Trend Detection
  • Customisable Interface

Who DiaCare is for

For Diabetics

  • Easy tracking
  • Smart reminders
  • Daily insights

For Parents

  • Real-time monitoring
  • Alerts for highs/lows
  • Peace of mind

For Healthcare professionals

  • Clean data summaries
  • Trend analysis
  • Secure patient access

How the DiaCare Ecosystem Works.

  • DiaCare (Main App): Central all-in-one app for daily diabetes tracking, reminders, insights, and personal management.
  • DiaWeb: Web-based portal for viewing detailed trends, reports, and long-term data analysis on a larger screen
  • DiaFollow: Secure caregiver and clinician platform for permission-based monitoring, alerts, and shared care.
  • GlucoseShield: Educational game that teaches diabetes management skills through interactive, gamified challenges.
